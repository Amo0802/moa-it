From first call to
app store — here's how it works

A clear, transparent process so you always know what's happening and what comes next.

01
Discovery

We get to know your vision

Reach out and we'll schedule a consultation — via video call, phone, or messages, whatever suits you best. We'll discuss your app idea, target audience, required features, and overall goals. Before any details are shared, we sign an NDA to keep your idea fully protected.

Video call Phone call Messages NDA signed
02
Proposal

Transparent pricing, no surprises

Once we have a clear picture of your requirements, we put together a detailed proposal — scope, timeline, and a fixed price. We work to find a number that makes sense for both sides. A 50% deposit is required to begin, with the remaining 50% due on delivery.

03
Design

Your app takes shape in days

Once we're aligned on the proposal, our design team gets to work. Within a few days you'll receive a full mockup of your app — every screen, every interaction. You get two rounds of revisions to make sure everything looks and feels exactly right before we write a single line of code.

04
Contract & Kickoff

We sign, then we build

Happy with the design? We sign the contract and development begins. You'll have access to a live progress tracker so you can follow along at every stage — no radio silence, no guessing where things stand.

Signed contract Live progress tracker
05
Launch

Live on App Store & Google Play in under a month

Most apps are fully built, tested, and submitted to both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store in less than 30 days from contract signing. More complex projects take a little longer — we'll set a realistic timeline upfront so you're never left waiting without an explanation.

App Store Google Play Under 30 days
06
Ongoing Support

We don't disappear after launch

Your app is live — but our work doesn't stop there. We offer 24/7 customer support throughout development and after launch. Our monthly hosting & maintenance plan keeps your app updated, secure, and running smoothly so you can focus on your business.
